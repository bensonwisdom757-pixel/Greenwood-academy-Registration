Greenwood Academy is a leading co-educational institution dedicated to nurturing excellence in academics, character, and creativity. Established in 2008, the school has built a strong reputation for providing quality education in a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment. Our mission is to inspire students to reach their full potential and become lifelong learners who contribute positively to society. At Greenwood Academy, we combine a robust Nigerian and British curriculum to ensure our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for success both locally and internationally. Our modern facilities include well-equipped science and computer laboratories, a state-of-the-art library, creative arts studios, and spacious classrooms designed for effective learning. The school prides itself on its team of highly qualified teachers who are passionate about education and dedicated to the holistic development of each child. We emphasize moral values, leadership, and community service, fostering a culture of respect, integrity, and excellence among our students. Beyond academics, Greenwood Academy offers a wide range of extracurricular activities such as music, sports, debate, robotics, and entrepreneurship clubs, helping students discover and develop their unique talents. Our vision is to raise globally minded leaders who are confident, compassionate, and committed to making a difference.
